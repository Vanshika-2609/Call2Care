Speech to Symptom Diagnosis

Call2Care is an AI-powered telemedicine tool that listens to your concerns, analyzes your speech, and detects possible symptoms. Our goal is to guide you towards timely diagnosis and the right medical attention.

How to Get Diagnosis

Step 1: Record your concerns in an audio file.

Step 2: Upload the file above and click submit.

Step 3: Receive AI-based diagnosis suggestions and recommended tests before visiting a doctor.
